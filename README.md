# Popcrush Project 2016
A free web collection of music from around the world.

## Composition
This collection is a blend of two music collections from the different time periods.

### Music from the period 2015-2017
This music collection contains songs that are discovered on the period from the late 2015 until the end of 2017 (median year 2016). Added NEW artists, previously unmentioned[^1], factually unmentioned[^2] or unheard of[^3], related artist[^4] or not: Ariana Grande (related artist: Leon Thomas III, where related: Victorious cast), T-PAIN (previously unmentioned in Pitbull - Hey Baby song), Taylor Swift, Wiz Khalifa (previously unmentioned in Leon Thomas III - Hello How Are You song), Alan Walker, Drake, DNCE, Eric Saade, Calvin Harris (previously unmentioned in Rihanna - We Found Love song), Chainsmokers, Katharine McPhee, Zara Larsson, Zendaya, etc.
[^1]: Included in the song from the previous package of same domain, but not mentioned.
[^2]: Included in the song from the package of another content domain or same context, but not mentioned.
[^3]: Leading singer that was NEWLY added, missing in previous package of same domain.
[^4]: Not being mentioned anywhere in the song in which the related artist took part.
